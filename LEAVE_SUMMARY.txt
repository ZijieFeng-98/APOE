WHAT THE ANALYSIS DOES
================================================================================

For each patient:
1. Align WGS reads to reference genome (BWA-MEM)
2. Sort and index alignment (SAMtools)
3. Extract APOE gene region (chr19:45409039-45412650)
4. Calculate coverage at key SNPs (rs429358, rs7412)
5. Determine APOE genotype (ε2, ε3, or ε4)
6. Assess Alzheimer's disease risk
7. Generate detailed report

For the cohort:
1. Aggregate all individual results
2. Calculate genotype frequencies
3. Perform statistical analysis
4. Compare to expected frequencies
5. Generate comprehensive summary
